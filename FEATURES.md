# OneMartGo - Complete Feature List

## 🎯 Executive Summary

OneMartGo is a **production-ready, launch-grade** B2C + B2B2C marketplace platform with **runtime-configurable providers**. Built for the Indian market with a ₹50 Lakh budget scope.

---

## 🌟 Unique Features (Competition Differentiators)

### 1. 🔄 **Runtime Provider Switching**
**The platform's flagship feature.**

- Change payment gateways **without code changes**
- Switch SMS/Email/Storage providers **without redeployment**
- All changes **take effect immediately**
- Credentials **AES-256 encrypted** in database
- **Zero downtime** during provider switches

**Business Value:**
- Negotiate better rates with providers
- Switch instantly if provider has downtime
- Test multiple providers without technical work
- Complete vendor independence

### 2. 🔗 **Reseller Ecosystem**
**Meesho-style social commerce**

- Any customer can become a reseller
- Generate unique product links with custom margins
- **White-label shipping** (reseller's name on package)
- Click tracking and conversion analytics
- Commission management and payouts
- WhatsApp/Telegram/Instagram integration

**Business Value:**
- Viral growth through social sharing
- Zero marketing cost customer acquisition
- Resellers become brand ambassadors

### 3. 🛍️ **Smart Product Discovery**
**Myntra-level shopping experience**

- AI-powered personalized feed
- Algolia search with typo-tolerance
- Advanced filters and facets
- Voice search (mobile)
- Visual similarity search
- Recently viewed tracking
